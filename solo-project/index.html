    Mission: The mission is to increase the number of Nigerian exporters through training, guidance, and off-taking, thereby minimizing trade gaps between Nigeria and other European country.

Inside farming

Inside farming, also known as indoor farming, is a growing trend in agriculture.It involves farming inside a building, which can take many forms, from flood trays to towers of greens, and from warehouses to basements. This method of farming does not require large equipment like tractors or combines, and often uses less water than growing outdoors. Instead, it relies on other types of technology, such as cameras and software, to grow food.
